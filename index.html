Unidos Financial Solutions

Mobile App Design / Responsive Development

Unidos is an innovative and fast-growing company providing alternative financial services to under-banked and unbanked consumers in the United States and abroad. Unidos was looking for a new self service application for its customers to be able to transfer money, pay bills and top up phone services on demand.

As the lead designer and frontend developer, I worked with Unidos to create a user friendly application their customers will enjoy. At the end of Q3 2015, the self service applications frontend is wrapped up and the backend work is begining. Unidos has been very impressed with the application and because of it they asked us to design and develop a new corporate site for them as well, which I will also help lead.

Graham Media Group

Website Design / Responsive Development

Graham Media Group is a small broadcast station group in five major markets, KPRC–Houston, WDIV–Detroit, KSAT–San Antonio, WKMG–Orlando, and WJXT–Jacksonville. Their stations provide millions of users with up-to-date breaking news, weather and community news on their market-leading websites.

As the lead designer and frontend developer, I worked with GMG to help provided a responsive design that would meet their needs. The design emphasizes large images and videos, integrated social media, and a customizable article filter. Since the launch of www.ksat.com in Q3 2015, the new design has gotten great feedback from their users and has been very well accepted. As a result of the design’s success, we are in process of pushing it across all station sites.

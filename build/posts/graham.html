Graham Media Group

Responsive Website: Design / Development
Back to All Work

Graham Media Group is a small broadcast station group in five major markets, KPRC–Houston, WDIV–Detroit, KSAT–San Antonio, WKMG–Orlando, and WJXT–Jacksonville. Their stations provide millions of users with up-to-date breaking news, weather and community news on their market-leading websites.

As the lead designer and frontend developer, I worked with GMG to help provided a responsive design that would meet their needs. The design emphasizes large images and videos, integrated social media, and a customizable article filter. Since the launch of www.ksat.com in Q3 2015, the new design has gotten great feedback from their users and has been very well accepted. As a result of the design’s success, we are in process of pushing it across all station sites.
